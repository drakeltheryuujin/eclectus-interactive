Who We Are

Eclectus Interactive is a small technology consultancy based in Brooklyn, NY. We specialize in digital arts and digital media solutions. We work with a close network of skilled artists, creatives, strategists, and technologists.

What We Do

Our passion is to offer affordable technical services to non-profits, small businesses, and entreprenuers--especially ones working with tight budgets. We thrive on offering professional clients technical solutions that will represent who they are and what they do. We passionately believe that these services should be accessible and affordable for all. It doesn't make us the biggest bucks--but it sure beats working on boring, corporate projects!

Services

Our capabilities

Web & UI Design

No more ugly or restrictive websites created with online site builders! We'll help you prioritize technology solutions that will scale with your services.

Software Development

We offer custom software consulting and development services from concept to culmination! Our technology services are catered to businesses and organizations that serve everyday people.

Site Hosting

Save time and money with our all-in-one installation, configuration, performance monitoring, and security. We can handle your migrations, backup, and data recovery, too. All we is your domain!

Illustration

Enhance your presentations, brand identity, or marketing presence with vibrant illustrations, iconography and typography. We have experience with advertising campaigns, publishing, branding, and other creative projects.

3D Modeling & Printing

Have a concept you would like brought into the 3rd dimension! We can do that too! We can sculpt and print small runs of your model.

Video Editing & Motion Graphics

With years of experience in visual communication and television production work, we can design motion for your streaming video, digital advertising, and interactive media.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How much will production cost?

    Many print-based projects will start at $100. Web & software projects cost a minimum of $350. Video and modeling vary and may be billed hourly.

  • Do I need to buy my own domain and/or hosting?

    We can provide domain and hosting that will be bundled into the overall project costs at a discounted rate. A flat rate fee will be applied to the price that covers hosting for a full year. Much more user-friend than GoDaddy.

  • How does the billing work?

    Flat rate projects require 50% upfront and 50% on project completion. Most projects will be billed hourly.

  • Will you be responsible for content management or other updates?

    All projects are delivered with the intent of the end-user continuing maintenance on their own. Retainer work is a separate hourly or monthly fee.

  • What tools are used?

    Adobe Creative Suite, Blender, XCode, Visual Studio, Lulzbot, and more!
